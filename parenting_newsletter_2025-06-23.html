How parents can prevent child drownings in 2025—What the evidence says

Motherly – 2025-06-20

In the effort to keep our little ones safe, it's crucial to be aware that, as flagged by the CDC, drowning was the primary cause of unintentional injury-related deaths among 1 to 4-year-olds in the U.S in 2024. Familiarising children with water from a young age, while maintaining constant supervision near water bodies and during bath times can be beneficial. Additional safeguards could include installing barriers around pools, equipping youngsters with life jackets when near larger water bodies, and enrolling them in swimming lessons once they're developmentally suited.
