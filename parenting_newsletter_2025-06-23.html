Heat Rash: Causes, symptoms and effective treatments

MadeForMums – 2025-06-19

In this cheery yet practical MadeForMums article, a seasoned GP shares tips for recognising, treating and preventing heat rash in our little darlings, as well as advice on when it's time to ring the doctor. Key indicators to look out for include small red spots, often around the neck, shoulders and chest. The GP advises cool baths, loose clothing and strategic heat dodging to manage the condition, and to ring a GP if there are symptoms of fever or if the rash persists for a few days. Hitting the headlines as balmy weather tickles Britain, keep an eye out for these signs to ensure your tots stay cool as cucumbers.
